Moomin World Map

A custom Mapbox Studio basemap inspired by the whimsical world of Tove Jansson and my time in Helsinki.

Moomin World is a custom map designed in Mapbox Studio inspired by the three months I spent working in Helsinki, Finland and learning about Finnish culture. The project recreates the whimsical, colorful atmosphere of Tove Jansson's Moomin universe by blending soft illustrative textures, rounded forms, and playful symbology within a geographic framework.

I focused on mimicking the gentle, storybook quality of Moomin art while using mapping tools to bring the landscape to life. This basemap captures the charm of Finnish design and the joy I found exploring the places that shaped Jansson's work, turning my time in Finland into an imaginative and interactive cartographic world.
