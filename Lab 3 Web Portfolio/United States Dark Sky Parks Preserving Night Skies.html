United States Dark Sky Parks

A static map highlighting designated Dark Sky Parks across the United States.

This map visualizes designated US Dark Sky Parks with Bortle Class 3 skies or darker. Spatial analysis was completed in ArcGIS Pro, and final cartographic refinement was done in Adobe Illustrator.

The project highlights the importance of preserving landscapes that offer exceptional night-sky visibility. By showing where these parks are located and their surrounding environments, the map encourages greater awareness of light pollution and the ecological and cultural value of dark skies.

This work reflects my interest in environmental storytelling and the power of cartography to make scientific and ecological information more accessible to a broad audience.
