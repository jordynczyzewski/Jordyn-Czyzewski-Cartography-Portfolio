Stitching the Story of Textile Waste

An interactive StoryMap tracing textile production, consumption, and waste through global trade and policy.

Click on the image to view the StoryMap!

Stitching the Story is an interactive Esri StoryMap built in ArcGIS Online that visualizes the global flow of textiles from production to consumption to waste. It highlights how fast fashion shapes labor conditions, environmental impacts, and emerging circular policies in places such as California and New York.

I was inspired to create this project through my work as President of Re-Wear It Wisconsin, a student organization at UW Madison that promotes sustainable fashion and textile reuse.

The StoryMap combines narrative mapping, trade data, and policy analysis to make the textile system more visible and understandable, inviting viewers to reflect on their own consumption and consider more just and sustainable alternatives.
