About Me

Hello! I am Jordyn Czyzewski, a student at the University of Wisconsin Madison studying Landscape & Urban Studies, People Environment Geography, and Environmental Studies, with certificates in Global Health and Integrative Design of Built and Natural Environments.

My work explores how place, community, and sustainability intersect, informed by experience in mapping, cartographic design, and urban research. Learn more about my background and experience.

I am committed to supporting equitable, people centered communities and using spatial tools to make resources and information more accessible.

My background spans sustainability initiatives, community engagement, environmental communication, and international urban research, guided by the belief that design and decision making should strengthen both human wellbeing and ecological resilience.
